# Weekly Tech Innovation Update — 2026-01-18

## Major Headlines
- Indonesia temporarily blocks Grok over risks of non-consensual sexual deepfakes.
- Malaysia limits access to Grok over concerns about sexual content generation.
- Apple signs a multiyear deal with Google to use Gemini models and Google Cloud to power future Siri features.
- Google confirms Gemini will power a more personalized Siri and future Apple Intelligence features.
- Alphabet reaches a $4 trillion market capitalization.
- Meta confirms layoffs in Reality Labs and says savings will be reinvested into wearables.
- Meta closes three VR gaming studios and halts new development for its VR fitness app Supernatural.
- Reports say Meta and EssilorLuxottica are discussing expanding Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses production capacity.
- Google launches Personal Intelligence, a Gemini feature integrating Gmail, Photos, Search, and YouTube for paid users.
- A source alleges a former Thinking Machines Lab executive shared confidential company information before returning to OpenAI.
- OpenAI plans to test ads below ChatGPT replies for Free and Go-tier users in the US.
- OpenAI expands its ChatGPT Go subscription tier globally at $8 per month in the US.

## Regulation & Policy
- Elon Musk criticizes the Apple–Google Gemini partnership over concentration of power concerns.
- Google outlines privacy controls and user consent features in Personal Intelligence.
- OpenAI says ChatGPT ads will be clearly labeled and will not influence responses or expose conversations to advertisers.
